Guest Safety Guidelines

Before You Book

  • Read reviews from previous guests
  • Verify host response rate and rating
  • Check property amenities and house rules
  • Confirm exact location and neighborhood safety

During Your Stay

  • Keep communication within the Esona platform
  • Report any safety concerns immediately
  • Follow property rules and local regulations
  • Keep emergency contact information handy

Emergency Support

  • 24/7 customer support hotline
  • Local emergency contacts provided
  • Immediate assistance for safety issues
  • Rebooking support if needed
