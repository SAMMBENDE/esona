How Esona Works

Discover how easy it is to find and book your perfect vacation rental

For Travelers

1. Search & Discover

Browse thousands of unique properties worldwide. Use our advanced filters to find exactly what you're looking for - from cozy apartments to luxury villas.

  • Search by location, dates, and guest count
  • Filter by amenities, price range, and property type
  • View authentic photos and detailed descriptions
  • Read reviews from previous guests

2. Book with Confidence

Secure your perfect stay with our trusted booking system. All properties are verified and hosts are identity-checked for your safety.

  • Instant booking for most properties
  • Secure payment processing
  • Comprehensive host verification
  • 24/7 customer support

3. Enjoy Your Stay

Check in to your home away from home and enjoy your travels. Our hosts are there to help make your stay memorable.

  • Easy check-in process
  • Direct communication with hosts
  • Local recommendations and tips
  • Support throughout your stay

For Hosts

List Your Space

Create a listing with photos, description, and amenities. Set your availability and pricing.

Welcome Guests

Receive booking requests and communicate with guests before and during their stay.

Get Paid

Receive secure payments 24 hours after guest check-in. Track earnings in your dashboard.

Safety & Trust

Verified Properties

All listings go through our verification process to ensure authenticity and quality.

Identity Verified Hosts

All hosts complete identity verification including government ID and background checks.

Review System

Both guests and hosts can leave reviews, maintaining transparency and trust in our community.

24/7 Support

Our customer support team is available around the clock to help with any issues.

Secure Payments

All transactions are processed securely with industry-standard encryption and protection.

Host Protection

Comprehensive insurance coverage protects hosts' properties up to $1 million.

Booking Protection

Free Cancellation

Many properties offer free cancellation options. Check individual listing policies for details.

Property Guarantee

If your accommodation doesn't match the listing, we'll help you find alternative accommodation or provide a refund.

Emergency Support

24/7 emergency hotline for urgent issues during your stay, including rebooking assistance if needed.
