Terms & Conditions

  • Both referrer and referred friend must be new to Esona
  • Referred friend must complete a stay of $75 USD or more (before taxes and fees)
  • Travel credits are issued within 24 hours of completing the qualifying stay
  • Credits expire 1 year from the date of issue
  • Credits can be used towards future bookings but cannot be transferred or redeemed for cash
  • Limit of $500 in referral credits per calendar year
  • Esona reserves the right to modify or terminate this program at any time
  • Fraudulent activity or attempts to game the system will result in account suspension
