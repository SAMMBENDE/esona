Frequently Asked Questions

Do gift cards expire?

No, Esona gift cards never expire. The recipient can use them whenever they're ready to travel.

Can gift cards be used for any booking?

Yes, gift cards can be used for any property booking on Esona worldwide. They can also be combined with other payment methods.

What if the booking costs more than the gift card value?

The recipient can pay the difference using any accepted payment method. Multiple gift cards can also be combined.

Can I get a refund for an unused gift card?

Gift cards are non-refundable once purchased. However, they never expire so they can be used at any time.

What if my gift card code doesn't work?

Contact our customer support team with your gift card details and we'll help resolve any issues.
