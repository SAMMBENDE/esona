Cancellation Policy

Understanding our cancellation policies and refund options

Cancellation Policy Overview

We understand that travel plans can change. Our flexible cancellation policies are designed to provide you with options while being fair to our hosts.

Flexible Policy

Full refund: Cancel up to 24 hours before check-in
Partial refund: 50% refund for cancellations within 24 hours
Service fees: Always refundable if host cancels

Moderate Policy

Full refund: Cancel up to 5 days before check-in
50% refund: Cancel within 5 days but at least 24 hours before
No refund: Cancel within 24 hours of check-in

Strict Policy

Full refund: Cancel within 48 hours of booking and at least 14 days before check-in
50% refund: Cancel at least 7 days before check-in
No refund: Cancel within 7 days of check-in

Special Circumstances

Extenuating Circumstances

Full refunds may be available for unexpected events beyond your control:

  • Natural disasters or severe weather
  • Medical emergencies
  • Government travel restrictions
  • Transportation strikes or cancellations

Host Cancellations

If a host cancels your reservation:

  • Full refund of all payments
  • Assistance finding alternative accommodation
  • Additional compensation for short-notice cancellations
  • Host penalties to prevent future cancellations

How to Cancel Your Reservation

1

Access Your Trips

Log into your account and go to "Your Trips" section

2

Select Reservation

Find the reservation you want to cancel and click "Manage"

3

Cancel Booking

Click "Cancel reservation" and follow the prompts

4

Confirm Cancellation

Review refund amount and confirm your cancellation

Refund Processing

Processing Time

Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days to your original payment method

Payment Method

Refunds are issued to the same payment method used for the original booking

Confirmation

You'll receive email confirmation when your refund has been processed
