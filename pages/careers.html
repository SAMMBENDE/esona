Our Mission

At Esona, we believe that travel has the power to connect people, create understanding, and build bridges across cultures. We're building a platform that makes unique travel experiences accessible to everyone while empowering hosts to share their spaces and stories with the world.

Our Hiring Process

1

Application

Submit your application with resume and cover letter. We review every application carefully.

2

Phone Screen

Initial conversation with our recruiting team to learn about your background and interests.

3

Technical/Skills Assessment

Demonstrate your skills through a relevant exercise or technical interview with the hiring team.

4

Team Interviews

Meet with team members and stakeholders to discuss your experience and how you'd fit with the team.

5

Offer & Onboarding

If it's a mutual fit, we'll extend an offer and help you get started on your Esona journey.
