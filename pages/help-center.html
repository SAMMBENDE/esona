Getting Started

To find your ideal vacation rental:

  1. Enter your destination, dates, and number of guests in the search bar
  2. Use filters to narrow results by property type, price, or amenities
  3. Sort results by price, rating, or recommendations
  4. Click on properties to view more details and check availability

Our advanced search tools help you find exactly what you're looking for, whether it's a beachfront villa or a city apartment.

Step-by-step guide to setting up your Esona account and profile:

  1. Click the "Sign Up" button in the top-right corner
  2. Enter your email address and create a secure password
  3. Verify your email through the confirmation link we send
  4. Complete your profile with personal details and preferences
  5. Add a profile photo to build trust with hosts
  6. Set up your payment methods for faster booking

A complete profile increases your chances of booking approval from hosts.

Everything you need to know about booking your first stay with Esona:

  1. Find a property you love and check availability for your dates
  2. Review house rules, cancellation policies, and additional fees
  3. Click "Book Now" or "Request to Book" depending on the listing
  4. Complete payment details (you'll only be charged after confirmation)
  5. Communicate with the host through our messaging system
  6. Receive booking confirmation with check-in instructions

For the best experience, book properties with high ratings and responsive hosts.

Booking & Payments

Esona accepts the following payment methods:

  • Major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay (on iOS devices)
  • Google Pay (on Android devices)

To manage your payment information:

  1. Go to your Account Settings
  2. Select "Payment Methods"
  3. Add, remove, or update your payment options

All payments are securely processed with industry-standard encryption.

Need to change your reservation? Here's how:

  1. Go to "Trips" in your account
  2. Select the booking you want to modify
  3. Click "Change reservation"
  4. Adjust dates, guest count, or other details
  5. Submit your request to the host

To cancel a reservation:

  1. Go to "Trips" in your account
  2. Select the booking you want to cancel
  3. Click "Cancel reservation"
  4. Review the cancellation policy and any applicable fees
  5. Confirm cancellation

Note: Refund amounts depend on the property's cancellation policy and how far in advance you cancel.

Understanding our refund policy:

  • Refunds are processed according to the property's cancellation policy (Flexible, Moderate, or Strict)
  • Processing times vary by payment method (3-5 business days for credit cards, 1-2 days for PayPal)
  • Service fees may be non-refundable in certain cases

If you encounter a payment issue or dispute:

  1. First, contact the host through our messaging system
  2. If unresolved, open a case with Esona Support within 24 hours of check-in
  3. Provide documentation or evidence supporting your claim
  4. Our team will review and help mediate the situation

Esona's guest guarantee covers significant discrepancies between listing and actual accommodation.

Host Information

Learn how to list your property and start hosting guests:

  1. Click "Become a Host" in your account menu
  2. Provide details about your property (type, location, size, amenities)
  3. Upload high-quality photos (at least 5 recommended)
  4. Set house rules, availability calendar, and pricing
  5. Complete safety and legal requirements
  6. Submit your listing for review

Once approved, your property will be visible to millions of potential guests worldwide. Our hosting experts can provide personalized tips to maximize your listing's potential.

Tips for optimizing your property listing and attracting guests:

  • Photos: Use bright, well-composed images that showcase your property's best features
  • Title: Create a compelling, descriptive title that highlights unique selling points
  • Description: Be detailed but concise, emphasizing special amenities and nearby attractions
  • Pricing: Research comparable listings and consider seasonal adjustments
  • Instant Book: Enable this feature to attract guests who prefer immediate confirmation
  • Response Time: Reply to inquiries promptly to improve your listing ranking
  • Reviews: Encourage satisfied guests to leave positive feedback

Regularly update your calendar availability to prevent declined bookings, which can negatively impact your listing's visibility.

Understanding insurance coverage and protection for hosts:

  • Property Damage Protection: Coverage up to $1,000,000 for damage caused by guests
  • Liability Insurance: Protection against third-party claims of injury or property damage
  • Security Deposit: Option to require a refundable deposit from guests
  • Screening Tools: Access to guest reviews and verification features
  • 24/7 Support: Dedicated host support line for emergencies

To file a claim:

  1. Document the damage with photos and descriptions
  2. Contact the guest through our messaging system
  3. If unresolved, submit a formal claim within 14 days of checkout
  4. Provide receipts or estimates for repair/replacement costs

Note: Some exclusions apply. Review the full Host Protection terms in your account settings.
